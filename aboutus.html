Our Story

Recipes To Go is an ingredient and meal kit service. We promise to only provide quality recipes and ingredients every time. We take pride in allowing our customers to customise their meals any way they wish, and be able to remove any ingredients to avoid any food wastage. With 1.3 billion tonnes of food wasted every year, we are hoping Recipes To Go is able to eradicate this issue and raise awareness on this matter.

Our aim is to make such a complicated, tedious activity, such as deciding and coming up with meals, the easiest task of the day!








We support our hardworking customers who want to eliminate the time and effort taken to decide on and prepare a meal on the daily.
