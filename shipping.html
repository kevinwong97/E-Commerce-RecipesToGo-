Shipping

Please be advised we only deliver to Victoria at the moment.

Delivery fees are calculated at checkout. Please ensure fees and charges are taken note of accordingly before finalising a purchase

We endeavour to provide our customers with the freshest, best quality ingredients in the most timely manner possible.

After your order has been processed, please allow 1 business day for your order to be delivered within the Metro Victoria area, and 2 to 3 business days within the Rural Victoria area.

Allow extra delivery time for all your orders in case of any delays due to bad weather, peak period, or unforeseen circumstances. We are not able to control your delivery once the item has been handed over to Australia Post.
